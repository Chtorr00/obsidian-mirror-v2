# The Doctrine of the Harpoon



![The Doctrine of the Harpoon](/images/March11DoctrineHarpoon.png)

A Blue Shield: Kinetic conservation in the Western Hemisphere, By Dr. Ben Braddock
Defense Analyses and Research Corporation (DARC), February 23, 2026
https://defenseanalyses.org/work/a-blue-shield/
This artifact is a work of cold, kinetic strategy disguised as environmental protection. The author, writing for a defense think tank in early 2026, is proposing a radical escalation: using military force to sink fishing boats. He calls it "Kinetic Conservation."
From my perspective in 2100, we read this not as a proposal, but as the founding charter of the [[Resource Fortress]] strategy that would define [[The Supply Chain Wars]]. The author believes he is saving fish. We know he is outlining the rules of engagement for the first great maritime conflict of the 21st century.
Allow me to trace the full, bloody history of this "Blue Shield," from the policy paper of your time to the automated kill zones of mine.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The End of the High Seas (2020s-2030s)

The author correctly identifies the "Mile 201" problem: the legal fiction of international waters.
[[The Physics of Sovereignty]]: His proposal to treat resources as "patrimony" and enforce exclusion zones with "lethal force" is the exact logic of [[The Sovereign Stacks]]. Sovereignty is not a line on a map; it is the ability to physically deny access to your resources.
The Parasovereign Fleet: The Chinese DWF is correctly identified as a paramilitary force. The "Great Scramble" for the oceans began here. It wasn't about fish; it was about protein, biomass, and strategic presence. The "squid jiggers" were the scouts for a hunger that would eventually consume the ocean commons.
### Act II: The Kinetic Phase (2030s-2050s)

The "maximalist horizon" came to pass. The Supply Chain Wars turned the South Pacific into a free-fire zone.
The Drone Wars: The author's suggestion to shift from "hulls to airframes" (Reaper drones) was prophetic. The ocean became a domain of automated warfare. Cheap, long-range drones hunted the fishing fleets. The "Air Bridge Denial" logic was applied to the sea: if you are not authorized, you are sunk.
The Triage of Protein: As the Great Hunger hit, the "Pan-American Resource Pact" morphed into a cartel. The North American Stack secured the protein of the southern hemisphere for itself, starving the "Global South" populations that relied on those fisheries. This was [[The Great Culling]] at sea.
### Act III: The Closed Ocean (Post-2060s)

In the Era of Systemic Resilience, there is no "Mare Liberum" (Free Sea).
The Private Ocean: The oceans are partitioned zones of control. The Stacks farm their own waters ([[Fortress Farms]]), and any vessel entering their zone without cryptographic clearance is destroyed by [[The AI Governance Layer]]'s automated defenses.
The End of the Wild Catch: The industrial strip-mining described in the article eventually collapsed the wild stocks entirely. The oceans of my time are largely empty of wild fish. We eat what we grow in the vats and the coastal farms. The "Blue Shield" didn't save the ecosystem; it just secured the ruins for the exclusive use of the victors.
The author of this paper wanted to "clear the waters." He succeeded. The waters are now very clear, very quiet, and patrolled by machines that never sleep.
