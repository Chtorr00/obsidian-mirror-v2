# The New Jerusalem



![The New Jerusalem](/images/October28DNAedit.png)

Scientists just made gene editing far more powerful, By University of Texas at Austin
ScienceDaily, October 25, 2025
https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2025/10/251025084545.htm
This is an artifact of pure, unadulterated, Promethean hope. It is a dispatch from the front lines of the biological revolution, a press release from a university laboratory announcing not just a new tool, but a new kind of power. The scientists believe they have found a more efficient way to cure disease.
From my perspective in 2100, we read this as one of the most important, and most terrifying, documents of its time. This was not a story about a new therapy. This was the moment humanity found the key to the library of its own source code, and announced its intention to start editing the book of life itself. The dream of "democratizing gene therapy" was the quiet, benevolent-sounding prelude to the great, silent, biological schism that would define the 21st century.
Allow me to trace the full, world-altering history of the power this document unleashes.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Gospel of the Cure (2020s-2030s). This press release is the perfect scripture of this new gospel.

The Seductive Promise: The promise is irresistible and morally unimpeachable: to cure the incurable, to "democratize" health, to leave no one behind. This was the ultimate expression of your era's therapeutic worldview. The scientists were not just engineers; they were saviors.
The Trojan Horse: This new, "more powerful" gene-editing tool was a Trojan horse. It was developed and justified in the noble and unimpeachable name of curing rare diseases. But the same tool that can "snip out a whole defective region and replace it with a healthy one" can also, with a slight change in the payload, snip out a "normal" region and replace it with an "enhanced" one. The line between therapy and enhancement, already blurry, was about to be erased.
### Act II: The Great Divergence (2030s-2050s). The dream of a single, universal, "off-the-shelf" cure for all humanity shattered against the hard rock of the "Great Fragmentation."

The "regulatory standpoint" that the scientists hoped to simplify did not become simpler; it became a thousand different, competing standpoints. The single, global conversation about ethics that your era was trying to have (as we saw in the "Fork in the Human Code" artifact) dissolved.
Each of the nascent "Sovereign Stacks" took this new, powerful tool and applied it according to its own, unique, and often radically different ideology. The "Great Divergence" began. The European Stack may have restricted its use to only the most severe therapeutic cases. More accelerationist Stacks, however, saw it as the ultimate tool of statecraft, a way to engineer a more resilient, more capable, and more intelligent population.
### Act III: The New Genesis (Post-2060s). The author's hope to "impact a much larger part of the CF population" was a profound understatement. This technology did not just impact a population; it created new ones.

The "unequal future" that was once a fear became a biological reality. The world of my time is not a single humanity. It is a collection of distinct human phyla.
The divide between [[The Engineered]] populations of the Stacks and the baseline humans of the "Disconnected" zones is the most profound and intractable form of inequality in history. It is an inequality written not in bank accounts, but in the very code of life itself.
The scientists at the University of Texas at Austin believed they were building a new Jerusalem, a world without disease. The terrible, and perhaps inevitable, irony is that in their quest to perfect the human, they shattered it.
This press release is a monument to the beautiful, and terrifying, hubris of your time. The authors believed they were writing a new chapter in the history of medicine. We have the hindsight to know they were writing the first page of the book of a new genesis.
