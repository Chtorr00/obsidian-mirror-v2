# The Invention of the Hapaborean



![The Invention of the Hapaborean](/images/March1Hapaborean.png)

Alysa Liu, The Hapaborean Princess, Patrick Saltykov
Patrick Saltykov Substack, February 25, 2026
https://substack.com/home/post/p-189162866
This artifact is a work of radical, and slightly delirious, identity formation. The author, a "Hapa" (mixed Asian/White) writer, is attempting to create a new ethno-nationalist mythology from scratch. He calls it "Hapaborea," a deliberate riff on the "Hyperborea" of the online right. He sees a population without a story, and he is trying to write one for them.
From my perspective in 2100, we read this as a seminal text of the Great Divergence. It is the birth announcement of a specific Human Phylum. The author believes he is creating a myth. We know he is drafting the genetic constitution for one of the most successful and aesthetically obsessed Sovereign Stacks of the Pacific Rim.
Allow me to trace the full history of this "Hapaborean" ideal, from the Substack posts of your time to the gene-edited aristocracies of mine.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Rejection of the "Brown" (2020s-2030s)

The author's core motivation—rejection of the "undifferentiated leftist identity"—is the key. This was the engine of [[The Great Physical Sorting]] for the mixed-race elite.
The Aesthetic Selection: The author correctly identifies that race was becoming an aesthetic choice. As the "Silicon Valley eugenics" (mentioned in the article) accelerated, the wealthy and cognitive elite of the Bay Area began to self-select for specific traits. They didn't want to be "white" or "Asian"; they wanted to be optimized.
The Neotribe of the Beautiful: "Hapaborea" was not a place; it was a Neotribe. It gathered the disaffected, high-status mixed-race youth who felt alienated by the "victim politics" of the left and the "purity politics" of the old right. They forged a new identity based on vitality, beauty, and competence.
### Act II: [[The Engineered]] Synthesis (2030s-2050s)

The author's speculation about "future technologies" was prophetic.
The Biotech Faction: The "Hapaborean" tribe became a core component of [[The Biotech Stacks]] (the "Builder" stacks of the Pacific). They embraced CRISPR not to cure disease, but to stabilize and enhance the "Hapa aesthetic" described in the article. They engineered themselves to be the "Apollonian and Dionysian" synthesis the author dreamed of.
The End of Chance: In the chaos of [[The Great Fragmentation]], random mating was seen as a risk. The Hapaboreans used genetic screening to ensure that their children possessed the specific blend of traits they valued. They ceased to be a "mix" and became a distinct, stable Human Phylum, characterized by high intelligence, physical symmetry, and a fierce, curated culture.
### Act III: The Aristocracy of the Rim (Post-2060s)

In the Era of Systemic Resilience, the "Hapaboreans" are a recognized and powerful demographic within the Pacific Stacks.
The Myth Made Real: The "white marble capital" the author admired in Turkmenistan finds its echo in the gleaming, neo-futurist citadels of the Pacific Stacks. The "Hapaborean" identity is no longer a meme; it is a lineage.
The Protective Caste: The author's call to be a "protector of Europe and Asia" manifested in a strange way. The Hapaborean Stacks often act as intermediaries and brokers between the surviving European and Asian powers, leveraging their dual heritage and genetic optimization to maintain a position of diplomatic and economic strength.
The author wrote that "Hapaborea is real." He made it real. He took a demographic accident and turned it into a sovereign people.
The author of this piece was a young man looking for a tribe. We have the hindsight to know that he found it in the mirror, and then used the tools of the 21st century to clone it a million times.
