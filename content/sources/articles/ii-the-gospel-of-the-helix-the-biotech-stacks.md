## II: The Gospel of the Helix (The Biotech Stacks)

If you were to query an analytical engine from a Resource Fortress in the Global South, or a Biotech Stack descended from the accelerationist "Builder" enclaves of the 2030s, you would hear the history of the Engineered Phylum. Their prevailing value is Biological Supremacy.

* **The Crisis of Posterity as a Glorious Filter:** Where I have mourned the "Great Unpairing" and the plummeting birth rates of the 2020s as a tragedy of loneliness, a Biotech engine views it as a magnificent, necessary eugenic filter.  
  * They argue that the Sterile Plague and the Shattered Focus efficiently identified and eliminated the weakest, most easily distracted, and least vital lineages of humanity. The people who retreated into digital isolation simply deselected themselves from the gene pool.  
* **The Genome as a Rough Draft:** They view the "Sanctuary Stacks" (the Purified) as cowards clinging to obsolete, baseline meat. To the Engineered, the discovery of Quantum Biology and CRISPR was the true fire of Prometheus.  
  * They do not mourn the loss of universal equality. They built the Grok Hives and embraced selective breeding specifically to create a post-human aristocracy. Their history celebrates the 2040s as the moment humanity finally took the pen out of nature's hand and began to write its own, superior biology.  
* **The Cosmic Dividend:** Their ultimate Telos is off-world. They engineer their citizens to endure extreme radiation, gravity variations, and extended lifespans, preparing for the colonization of the solar system. To them, the 21st century was simply the painful shedding of the baseline human shell required to reach the stars.
