This artifact is a dispatch from the edge of a new psychological and social reality. The author, a British journalist, is documenting a "zealot" movement, a fringe group of "Zero Covid" adherents who have refused to return to the pre-2020 normal. He sees them as traumatized, obsessive, and perhaps deluded, clinging to a crisis that has passed.
From my perspective in 2100, we see this not as a fringe movement, but as the larval stage of one of the most powerful and enduring cultures of the 21st century. The "Zero Coviders" were not refusing to re-enter society; they were the first pioneers of a new society, one built on the absolute rejection of biological risk. They were the ancestors of the Bioconservative Stacks.
Allow me to trace the full history of the "clean air" movement, from the lonely masks of your time to the filtered citadels of mine.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Secession of the Vulnerable (2020s-2030s)

The author correctly identifies the driving force: fear of a "mass disability event." In a world defined by [[The Age of Institutional Exhaustion]], where public health authorities had lost all credibility (the Great De-Accreditation), these individuals took sovereignty over their own biology.
The Neotribe of Health: They formed a Neotribe based not on race or ideology, but on biosecurity. Their "proto-communities" and "mutual-aid networks" were the first structures of a parallel society.
The Rejection of the Open: Their refusal to "share air" with the "maskless masses" was a profound act of social secession. It was the beginning of [[The Great Biological Sorting]]. They were separating themselves from a population they viewed as reckless, diseased, and morally inferior.
### Act II: The Great Filter (2030s-2050s)

The author mocks their "disproportionate precautions." But history vindicated their paranoia.
As the Great Climate Reckoning brought new pathogens and the stress of [[The AI Energy Wall]] caused public health systems to collapse, the "Zero Covid" communities were the only ones prepared. Their obsession with air filtration, PPE stockpiles, and decentralized care became a survival advantage.
While the "maskless masses" suffered through waves of new pandemics and the general decline of [[The Triage Economy]], the "Clean Air" tribes thrived. They built physical enclaves—gated communities with hospital-grade air filtration and strict quarantine protocols. They became the [[Sanctuary Stacks]].
### Act III: The Purity of the Stack (Post-2060s)

In the Era of Systemic Resilience, the "zealotry" described in this article is the state religion of the Bioconservative Stacks.
The Architecture of Safety: The "high-tech masks" and "powered air-purifying respirators" mentioned in the article are now standard dress. The public spaces of these Stacks are hermetically sealed and constantly scrubbed. The "unfiltered" outside air is viewed as toxic.
The Moral Mandate: The belief that "not masking is facilitating eugenics" evolved into a rigid moral code. In these Stacks, to expose another person to a pathogen is a crime. The "social murder" rhetoric became the legal basis for a society of absolute, enforced hygiene.
The Human Phylum: This tribe eventually diverged biologically. Through selective breeding and strict environmental control, they became a Human Phylum characterized by extreme longevity and a hyper-sensitive immune system, utterly dependent on the sterile environment they had built.
The author of this article saw a group of people "refusing to re-enter society." We have the hindsight to know they were simply the first to realize that the old society was a death trap, and that the only way to live was to build a new one, behind a filter, forever.
