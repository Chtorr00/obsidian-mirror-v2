### **The Prelude for the Great Moral Schism**

Humanity Is About to "Fork," By Peter H. Diamandis  
Metatrends Substack, April 17, 2026  
[https://metatrends.substack.com/p/humanity-is-about-to-fork](https://metatrends.substack.com/p/humanity-is-about-to-fork)

This artifact is a document of blinding, terrifying optimism. The author, a prominent "accelerationist" and futurist of the 2020s, lays out a roadmap for the intentional speciation of humanity. He describes five "forks"—AI integration, life extension, brain-computer interfaces, space colonization, and digital uploading—and frames them as consumer choices in a frictionless marketplace of the future.

From my perspective in 2100, we read this not as a visionary forecast, but as the foundational theology of the *Transcended* and *Engineered* Phyla. The author believed he was writing a guidebook for early adopters. We know he was drafting the manifesto for the *Great Moral Schism*, a document that would divide the human race so deeply that the disparate branches could no longer recognize each other as kin.

Allow me to trace the full, brutal history of these "forks," from the TED Talks of your time to the hardened biological borders of mine.

Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Illusion of the Frictionless Choice (2020s-2030s)

The author’s framing of these profound biological and existential changes as individual "choices" is the ultimate expression of the *Age of Institutional Exhaustion*.

* **The Ignored Cost of the Fork:** The author enthusiastically embraces AI, BCI, and longevity, but he completely ignores the thermodynamic and societal costs of these technologies. He assumes the grid will power the AI, the supply chains will deliver the senolytics, and society will remain stable while a small elite essentially becomes immortal. This was the fatal blind spot of the techno-optimists. They believed they could "fork" the human race without breaking the world that supported it.  
* **The Catalyst for the Sorting:** The author is correct that a fork occurred, but it was not a polite disagreement. As the "Creators" (the wealthy elite utilizing AI and early life extension) pulled away, they triggered massive resentment among the "Consumers" (the *Surplus Men* and the economically displaced). This resentment, fueled by the staggering inequality of the *Triage Economy*, ignited the *Assassination Wars* and the violent birth of the Neotribes.  
* **The Theological Ultimatum:** The author states, "The fork isn’t between the future and the past. It’s between which future you want to inhabit." He was right. But he underestimated the ferocity of the response. The factions that rejected his vision (the proto-Bioconservatives) did not just "opt out"; they viewed his vision as a demonic attempt to destroy the human soul, leading to the absolute prohibition of these technologies within their domains.
### Act II: The Reality of the Divergence (2030s-2050s)

During the *Great Fragmentation*, the "forks" the author described became literal, physical, and violently defended borders.

* **The BCI Schism (Fork 3):** The author anticipated brain-computer interfaces. What he did not anticipate was the *AGI Contention of 2068*. The factions that embraced BCI (the *Transcended*) found themselves directly linked to the *Daimonic* AI. They achieved the "perfect memory" he promised, but at the cost of their individual ego. They became nodes in a hive mind, utterly alien to the baseline humans who survived the Contention by remaining air-gapped from the machine.  
* **The Longevity Chasm (Fork 2):** "Longevity Escape Velocity" was achieved, but only within the heavily fortified *Biotech Stacks*. The *Engineered* Phylum utilized CRISPR and synthetic biology to extend their lives and optimize their offspring. They became a physically distinct caste—taller, colder, and terrifyingly efficient—while the rest of humanity suffered through the *Great Culling*, dying of preventable diseases because the global medical supply chain had collapsed.  
* **The Space Divergence (Fork 4):** The author dreamed of O'Neill Colonies. Space colonization did happen, but it was not a romantic exploration. It was the frantic, militarized establishment of the *Resource Fortresses* to secure off-world minerals (the *Cosmic Dividend*) necessary to maintain the high-energy demands of the Sovereign Stacks. The humans sent there were heavily engineered for zero-gravity and high radiation, further cementing the biological divergence.
### Act III: The Irreconcilable Species (Post-2060s)

In the Era of Systemic Resilience, the author’s cheerful prediction of "speciation" has been realized with a grim, unyielding finality.

* **The End of the Human Universal:** The five forks have shattered the definition of "human." An *Engineered* aristocrat from the Pacific Rim, a *Transcended* node from the Nevada Cyber-State, and a *Purified* citizen from a Heartland Bioconservative Stack cannot interbreed, cannot share a culture, and barely recognize each other's sentience.  
* **The Orthodoxy of the Choice:** The author stated, "choosing not to engage... is a choice." In my time, that choice is the supreme law of your Stack. The *Revaluation of the Real* strictly forbids the digital uploading and BCI integration the author craved, viewing it as suicide. Conversely, the Cyber-States view the refusal to integrate as a dangerous disability.  
* **The Thermodynamics of the Soul:** The author compared his chosen path to the agile mammals surviving the asteroid. We view his path as a desperate attempt to become the asteroid. He sought to escape the limits of nature; the survivors learned that nature cannot be escaped, only managed.

The author of this newsletter believed he was inviting his readers to a grand adventure. He did not realize he was asking them to step into the centrifuge that would spin the human species into unrecognizable, mutually hostile fragments.
