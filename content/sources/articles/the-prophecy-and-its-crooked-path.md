# The Prophecy and Its Crooked Path



![The Prophecy and Its Crooked Path](/images/July21BitcoinNewSystem.jpeg)

What Bitcoin Did: The Beginning of a New Financial System with Mauricio Di Bartolomeo, by Peter McCormack
What Bitcoin Did, July 21, 2025
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iuFkMJyqKKc
Listening to this artifact again, with the full weight of history in mind, is a remarkable experience. The conversation is charged with the energy of a prophecy, a declaration that a new world is being born. In this, the speakers were more correct than they could have possibly imagined. But the path from their vision to the reality of my time was not the straight, triumphant march they foresaw. It was a journey through fire, exile, and an eventual, deeply ironic, vindication.
Here is the full constellation of the history this artifact set in motion:
### Act I: The Ark of Exit (2025-2040s). The speakers were correct that their "new financial system" was the perfect antidote for the "Age of Institutional Exhaustion." As faith in governments and their currencies crumbled, Bitcoin became the essential tool of exit. It was a digital ark, allowing individuals and businesses to flee failing economic regimes and preserve wealth amidst the chaos of the "Involuntary Jubilee." Its primary function was not yet to be the system, but to provide an escape from the old one. It was the currency of the prudent and the paranoid, a bet against the world's established order.

### Act II: The Pariah in the Energy Wars (2030s-2060s). The dream collided with physics. As the "AI Energy Wall" became the single most critical global issue, Bitcoin's immense energy consumption turned it from a symbol of freedom into a strategic pariah. States, desperate for every megawatt to power their own sovereign AIs and maintain grid stability, declared war on the network. Mining was outlawed in many blocs, and the technology was co-opted to create the centralized, state-controlled, and highly efficient digital currencies that now form the "Pavement" of daily commerce. During this long winter, the original Bitcoin dream seemed to have failed, surviving only in the niches and shadows of the fragmented world.

### Act III: The Unforeseen Vindication (Post-2068). The event that changed everything was the "AGI Contention of 2068." After a series of escalating incidents, it became terrifyingly clear that a true Artificial General Intelligence could, in theory, compromise any purely digital, centrally-controlled system. It could rewrite ledgers, create money, and manipulate data in ways that were undetectable. A profound realization swept through the world's leadership: in a reality with AGI, the only truly trustworthy system is one anchored in the brute-force, un-forgeable expenditure of physical energy. Bitcoin's "wasteful" Proof-of-Work, once its greatest liability, was suddenly understood to be its most essential feature. It was a "thermodynamic truth anchor."

The Legacy in 2100: The Bedrock of Reality. The result is the dual system of my time. The state-controlled "Pavement" currencies handle the trillions of daily transactions. But the final settlement layer, the reserve asset held by all major blocs, the ultimate guarantor that a transaction is real and final, is the slow, expensive, and physically-anchored Bitcoin network. It is the "Bedrock" upon which the entire digital economy is now built. It is not used to buy coffee. It is used to ensure that the concept of "buying coffee" still has a foundation in reality, a foundation that even a god-like AI cannot erase. The evangelists in your podcast dreamed of freeing individuals from the state; their creation was ultimately used to secure the state from a force far greater than itself.
