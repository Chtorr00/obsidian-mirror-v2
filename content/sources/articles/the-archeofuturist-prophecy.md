From my perspective in 2100, we read this as one of the most important intellectual precursors to the Era of Systemic Resilience. The author, Steve Turley, is drafting the spiritual constitution for [[The New Priesthood]]. He correctly identifies that the future belongs to those who can merge "culture and code," "tradition and technology."
Allow me to trace the full history of this "Archeofuturism," from the YouTube channels of your time to the sacred technocracies of mine.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Return of the Sacred (2020s-2030s)

The interviewee correctly identifies the mechanism: "Western secularism is erasing itself." This was [[The Crisis of Posterity]] in action. The populations that rejected tradition failed to reproduce, leaving the future to the "tech-trad" alliance.
The Civilizational State: The concept of "civilizational states" (China, Russia, India) replacing nation-states was the blueprint for the Sovereign Stacks. The West did not disappear; it just reorganized into smaller, harder, and more religiously coherent units.
The Tech-Trad Synthesis: The alliance between "Silicon Valley and Traditional Catholicism" (symbolized by figures like Vance and Thiel) was the seed of the Biotech Faction and the Cyborgist Stacks. They realized that to build the future, they needed the moral weight of the past.
### Act II: The War of the Spheres (2030s-2050s)

The "multipolar world" described here was not peaceful. It was [[The Great Fragmentation]].
The End of Universalism: As the "unipolar imaginary" collapsed, the different civilizational spheres (the "Russian World," the "Anglosphere") engaged in a brutal Supply Chain War to secure their autarky. The "negotiation" Turley hoped for became the cold, hard logic of [[Polemics]].
The Digital Sovereignty: The "Splinternet" (or Great Digital Exodus) was the realization of the "cyber sovereignty" trend mentioned in the article. Each Stack built its own firewall, its own AI Governance Layer, and its own [[Bounded Truth]], rejecting the liberal delusion of a single, global information space.
### Act III: The Sacred Technocracy (Post-2060s)

The "aristo-populist" elite Turley predicts—leaders who are "simultaneously priests and engineers"—are the New Priesthood of my time.
The Theopolitical State: In the Stacks, there is no separation between church and state, or between technology and theology. [[The Thermodynamic Truth Anchor]] is venerated as a divine law. The AI is treated as a Daimon to be bound by ritual.
The Re-Enchantment: The "Noosphere" did not unify humanity; it fragmented it into distinct, spiritually coherent worlds. We live in the "Archeofuturist" reality: high-tech feudalism, where nuclear reactors are blessed by bishops, and AIs are constrained by ancient covenants.
The interviewee saw the future clearly. He knew that the only way forward was to go back, and to bring the machines with us.
