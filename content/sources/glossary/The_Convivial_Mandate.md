# The Convivial Mandate

**Timeline:** 2050 - Present

The set of social and technological laws governing the Sovereign Stacks, designed to protect human cognition and relationships from the corrosive effects of addictive technology. Born from the trauma of the Sterile Plague and the Shattered Focus, the Mandate restricts the use of AI and digital media in public spaces and childhood development. It prioritizes "convivial" tools—those that enhance human autonomy and connection—over tools that induce dependency or isolation.
