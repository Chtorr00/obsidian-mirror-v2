# The Thermodynamic Truth Anchor

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

The integration of the Bitcoin network into the foundation of global governance and AI safety. Following the Great De-Accreditation and the AGI Contention, civilization realized that "truth" could no longer be established by human institutions or easily-forged digital signatures. The Anchor uses Proof-of-Work to tie digital records to the unforgeable, costly expenditure of physical energy. It is the bedrock of the Transparent Ledger and the restraint system for AGI.
