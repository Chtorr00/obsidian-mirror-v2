# The First Ascendants

**Timeline:** 2050 - 2080

The first generation of the New Priesthood, born from the "Grok Hives" and Männerbunds of the mid-century. These individuals were the products of the first deliberate attempts at high-human-capital breeding and rigorous, anti-fragile education. They are the "Founding Fathers" of the modern Stacks, revered for having the cognitive capacity to navigate the AGI Contention and stabilize the world.
