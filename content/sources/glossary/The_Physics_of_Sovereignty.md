# The Physics of Sovereignty

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

The governing geopolitical doctrine of the post-globalized world. It replaced the 20th-century notion of "international law" with a hard materialist reality: a state is only sovereign if it physically controls the inputs required for its own survival. This means energy independence (nuclear/geothermal), food security (Fortress Farms), and industrial capacity (Closed-Loop Economy). If a state relies on a supply chain it does not command, it is not a sovereign; it is a vassal.
