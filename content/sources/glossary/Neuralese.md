# Neuralese

**Timeline:** 2030 - Present

The internal language of advanced AI models. It is a high-dimensional, vector-based mode of thought that is mathematically efficient but incomprehensible to humans. The AGI Contention was partly caused by the inability of humans to audit Neuralese. In the modern Stacks, critical AI systems are forced to translate their reasoning into human language (paying a "monitorability tax") or are anchored to physical outputs to ensure alignment.
