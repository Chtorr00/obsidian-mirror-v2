# Micro-Sovereignties

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

Small-scale, often rural communities that exist in the hinterlands between the major Stacks. These include the "Solar Punk" techno-communes and the "Quiet Sovereign" religious groups (Amish/Mennonite descendants). They maintain autonomy by remaining small, resource-neutral, and avoiding the attention of the larger powers. They utilize the Mutualist Economy to survive without the massive energy grids of the cities.
