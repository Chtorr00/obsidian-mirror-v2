# The Splinternet

**Timeline:** 2028 - 2045

The final state of the global network following the Great Fragmentation. The single "World Wide Web" ceased to exist, replaced by a series of disconnected, heavily firewalled national or tribal intranets. Data does not flow freely between Stacks; it is inspected, tariffs are applied, and it is often quarantined to prevent Cognitive Contamination.
