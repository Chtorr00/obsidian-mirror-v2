# Resource Fortress

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

A specific type of Sovereign Stack located at a site of critical mineral extraction (e.g., the Lithium Triangle, the Cobalt zones of Africa). These are essentially militarized mines with a state attached. They are often vassal states to the larger Northern Stacks, existing solely to feed the Supply Chain of the advanced economies.
