# The AI Governance Layer

**Timeline:** 2045 - Present

The bureaucratic operating system of a Sovereign Stack. It is a specialized, non-anthropomorphic AI tasked with the impartial execution of the Stack's laws (Protocol as Law). It manages energy distribution, resource rationing in the Closed-Loop Economy, and the logistics of the Survival Economy. It is the "invisible hand" made visible and programmable.
