# Systemic Humanism

**Timeline:** 2050 - Present

The moral philosophy that guides the New Priesthood. It posits that human beings are not individuals in a vacuum, but nodes in a complex, interdependence system (family, guild, stack). It values the health of the system over the autonomy of the part. It is the ethical framework that justifies the Closed-Loop Economy and the strict social controls of the Era of Systemic Resilience.
