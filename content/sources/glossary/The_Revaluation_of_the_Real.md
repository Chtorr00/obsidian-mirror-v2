# The Revaluation of the Real

**Timeline:** 2040 - Present

The cultural and spiritual reformation that defines the Era of Systemic Resilience. Born as an immune response to the Great Contamination and the Sterile Plague, this philosophy places supreme value on physical reality, biological life, and embodied risk. It rejects the "frictionless" digital pleasures of the 2020s in favor of "costly" real-world achievements. It is the reason the Stacks privilege manual competence, face-to-face community, and unedited human art.
