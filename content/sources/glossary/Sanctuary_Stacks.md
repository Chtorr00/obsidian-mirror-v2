# Sanctuary Stacks

**Timeline:** 2040 - Present

The Sovereign Stacks formed by the Bioconservative factions (e.g., the "Zero Covid" descendants). They are defined by extreme environmental purity, air filtration, and the rejection of synthetic biology and GMOs. They view the outside world as biologically toxic and maintain the strictest quarantine protocols of all the Stacks.
