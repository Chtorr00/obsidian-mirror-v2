# The Sacred Duty

**Timeline:** 2040 - Present

The cultural imperative within the Stacks to reproduce. Rejecting the "choice" model of the 20th century, the Stacks view procreation as a service to the collective. Status, housing, and resources are often tied to family size. It is the spiritual reversal of the Crisis of Posterity.
