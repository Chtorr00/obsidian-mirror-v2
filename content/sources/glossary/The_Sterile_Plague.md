# The Sterile Plague

**Timeline:** 2023 - 2045

The psychological epidemic that decimated the birth rates of the developed world. It was characterized by the mass substitution of human relationships with synthetic alternatives—AI companions, infinite pornography, and parasocial digital interactions. It was the primary driver of the Great Unpairing, creating a demographic collapse that wiped out the secular/liberal populations of the pre-collapse era.
