# The Artisan

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

The primary human worker of the Purpose Economy. In a world where AI handles all data processing and logistics, the only labor with value is that which requires biological accountability, physical dexterity, or high-stakes judgment. Artisans are the engineers who fix the nuclear reactors, the soldiers who patrol the borders, and the artists who create High Art. They are organized into Guilds and are the moral backbone of the Stack.
