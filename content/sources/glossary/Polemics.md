# Polemics

**Timeline:** 2055 - Present

The formal, machine-mediated statecraft that governs relations between the Sovereign Stacks. Because the Stacks possess incompatible values and Bounded Truths, traditional human diplomacy (based on persuasion and shared norms) is impossible. Polemics is a system of high-stakes, game-theoretic protocols negotiated by the AI Governance Layers of rival Stacks. It prevents war by ensuring mutually assured economic or physical destruction for treaty violations, verified by the Architecture of Pervasive Security.
