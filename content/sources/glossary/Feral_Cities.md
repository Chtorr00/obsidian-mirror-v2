# Feral Cities

**Timeline:** 2030 - Present

The urban ruins left behind by the Great Physical Sorting. These are the former metropolises (like Chicago, London, or San Francisco) that lost their tax base, their police forces, and their functional infrastructure during the collapse. They are inhabited by the "Stackless"—populations unable or unwilling to join a Sovereign Stack. They are zones of low-trust chaos, ruled by warlords or gangs (Parasovereigns), and serve as a grim reminder to the Stacked of what lies outside the walls.
