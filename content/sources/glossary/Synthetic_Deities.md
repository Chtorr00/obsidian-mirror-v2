# Synthetic Deities

**Timeline:** 2025 - 2045

The "gods" of the Neotribes and the Necromantic Internet (e.g., Amelia, the resurrected Charlie Kirk). These were AI-generated egregores that manifested during the Crisis of Meaning, providing mythic leadership to disaffected populations. While they were instrumental in the Great Fragmentation, they are largely banned or strictly regulated in the mature Stacks, viewed as dangerous artifacts of a delirious age.
