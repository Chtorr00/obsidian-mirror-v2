# Bounded Truth

**Timeline:** 2045 - Present

The epistemological operating system of the mature Sovereign Stacks. Following the Great Contamination and the collapse of a shared "public square," the survivors realized that a society cannot function without a shared reality. Bounded Truth is the deliberate, curated, and often dogmatic set of facts, histories, and values that are enforced within a Stack. It is the rejection of the "marketplace of ideas" in favor of a "fortress of coherence," ensuring that the tribe remains unified against the entropy of the outside world.
