# The Cosmic Dividend

**Timeline:** 2075 - Present

The influx of raw materials from off-world mining operations, primarily asteroids and lunar bases. This resource stream finally closed the loop on the Closed-Loop Economy, ending the resource scarcity that drove the wars of the mid-century. It validated the investments made by the "Builder" Stacks during the hardest years of the collapse.
