# The Purpose Economy

**Timeline:** 2050 - Present

The upper tier of the dual economy within a Sovereign Stack. While the Survival Economy guarantees life, the Purpose Economy provides meaning. It is the domain of status, luxury, and social capital, accessible only through contribution to the tribe. It is the answer to the Crisis of Meaning, ensuring that even in a post-scarcity automated world, human ambition has a ladder to climb.
