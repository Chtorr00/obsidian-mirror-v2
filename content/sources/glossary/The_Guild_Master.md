# The Guild Master

**Timeline:** 2045 - Present

The highest rank within the Guilds of the Purpose Economy. In the absence of university accreditation, the Guild Master is the ultimate arbiter of competence. They are the only ones authorized to certify an Artisan. Their authority comes not from a degree, but from a lifetime of verifiable work recorded on the Transparent Ledger and their ability to judge character in a way AI cannot.
