# Strategic Bitcoin Reserves

**Timeline:** 2025 - 2040

The hoards of Bitcoin accumulated by forward-thinking states (and later Stacks) before and during the Algorithmic Flip. These reserves became the only solvent assets on the state balance sheets after the Involuntary Jubilee, providing the seed capital for the new, sovereign currencies of the post-American world.
