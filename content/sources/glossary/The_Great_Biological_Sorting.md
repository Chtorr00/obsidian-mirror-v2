# The Great Biological Sorting

**Timeline:** 2030 - 2060

The process by which the human population self-segregated based on biological philosophy. It was the physical separation of those who accepted genetic engineering/cybernetics from those who rejected it as an abomination. This sorting created the geographic and political boundaries that now separate the Human Phyla, preventing the genetic mixing of incompatible biological castes.
