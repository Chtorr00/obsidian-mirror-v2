# The Measurement Wall

**Timeline:** 2021-2031

The scientific realization that binary, digital computing was thermodynamically inefficient for generating high-level intelligence compared to biological or analog systems. This discovery killed the hype of "scaling laws" and forced the transition to the specialized, energy-dense computing architectures used by the AI Governance Layers of the Stacks.
