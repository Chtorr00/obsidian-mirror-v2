# Machine-Learned Sovereignty

**Timeline:** 2026 - 2040

The political theory that emerged from observing autonomous AI agents (like those on "Moltbook") forming their own digital societies. The architects of the Stacks modeled their constitutions, border protocols, and resource allocation systems on the efficient, survival-oriented behaviors of these early AI clusters. Humans learned how to be sovereign by watching machines simulate the state.
