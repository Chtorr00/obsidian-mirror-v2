# The Council of Ghosts



![The Council of Ghosts](/images/October30WEF.png)

World Economic Forum, October 16, 2025
https://www.weforum.org/press/2025/10/annual-meetings-of-the-global-future-councils-and-cybersecurity-2025-resilience-and-future-ready-solutions/
This artifact is a dispatch from the high summer of 20th-century optimism, a gathering of its most capable minds . The World Economic Forum has convened its Global Future Councils, a collection of over 500 leaders from government, business, and civil society, to create "future-ready solutions" for the world's most complex problems . They speak of "dialogue," "collective action," and "global cooperation" as the keys to a secure and prosperous future .
From my perspective in 2100, we read this document not as a plan for the future, but as a group portrait of the last, great, sincere generation of a dying order. It is a perfect artifact of the "Age of Institutional Exhaustion" . These are the world's pilots, earnestly debating the flight plan, unaware that the engines are on fire and the wings are about to fall off.
Allow me to trace the full, tragic history of the beautiful and irrelevant ideas this council championed.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
Act I: The Illusion of Control (2020s-2030s). This meeting was the high-water mark of the 20th-century elite. They genuinely believed that "dialogue" and "public-private collaboration" were the tools to manage the 21st century . Their entire framework was built on the assumption of a stable, global, cooperative order. This was their fatal blind spot. They were attempting to apply the tools of institutional consensus-building at the very moment those institutions were losing all legitimacy . They were trying to steer a ship that was already breaking apart in the first swells of the "Great Fragmentation" .
Act II: The Great Nullification (2030s-2050s). The "future-ready solutions" this council proposed were nullified, not by debate, but by physics and social collapse.
The "AI Energy Wall": Their enthusiastic belief in "AI as a key driver of future growth" and a "new oil" ran directly into a physical wall . The speculative bubble burst, not because of bad policy, but because the energy cost of their brute-force scaling models became infinite, triggering the "AI Winter" and a catastrophic economic correction .
The Moral Vacuum: Their focus on "cybersecurity" and "digital trust" was a tragic misdiagnosis . They saw a technical problem of "resilience" , but the true crisis was moral and epistemic. The digital world was not collapsing from a lack of "aligned frameworks" ; it was being torn apart by "semiotic warfare" and the "competing, irreconcilable moral frameworks" of the "Moral Vacuum" . They were trying to build stronger locks while the concept of "private property" was dissolving.
Act III: The New Sovereigns (Post-2060s). The world of my time, the "Era of Systemic Resilience" , was not built by a global council of "thought leaders" . It was built by the survivors of the collapse, from the bottom up. The problems of the 2020s were not "solved." They were rendered obsolete.
"Global cooperation" was replaced by the cold, pragmatic, protocol-based statecraft of the "Constellation," the "Patchwork States" that rose from the ashes.
The "AI" they dreamed of was replaced by the mature "AI Governance Layer," a system anchored not by "dialogue," but by the hard physical constraints of energy and the "Planetary Boundaries framework" .
The men and women in Dubai were the best and brightest of their time, and this artifact is their testament. They saw the future through the "rearview mirror" of their own 20th-century triumphs, blind to the fact that the "windshield" they were looking through was a mirage . They were the last, great optimists, drawing a beautiful, useless map for a world that was already gone.
