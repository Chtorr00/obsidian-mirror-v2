### **The Taxonomy of the Void**

The LGBTQ Tidal Wave Was Never About a Social Contagion, By Adam Ellwanger  
The American Mind, March 20, 2026  
[https://americanmind.org/salvo/the-lgbtq-tidal-wave-was-never-about-a-social-contagion/](https://americanmind.org/salvo/the-lgbtq-tidal-wave-was-never-about-a-social-contagion/)

This artifact is a fascinating piece of forensic sociology, written at the exact moment the pendulum of your cultural history reached its apex and began its violent, downward swing. The author is attempting to explain a sudden, massive demographic reversal: the sharp decline in youth claiming non-normative gender and sexual identities. He correctly dismisses the "social contagion" theory, arguing instead that this was a strategic, rational adaptation to the "moral arithmetic" of a society that had weaponized identity.

From my perspective in 2100, we read this not merely as a critique of "woke culture," but as the definitive diagnostic report on the terminal stages of the Moral Vacuum. The youth of the 2020s were not suffering from a virus; they were navigating a collapsing social structure where the only remaining currency was the taxonomy of oppression.

Allow me to trace the full, brutal history of this "dark calculus," from the fluid identities of your time to the hardened biological realities of mine.

Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Calculus of the Moral Vacuum (2020s-2024)

The author’s identification of "intersectionality" as a mechanism of social survival is historically flawless. In the Age of Institutional Exhaustion, as traditional avenues for status, homeownership, and economic mobility were sealed off, the youth sought refuge in the only hierarchy left available to them.

* **The Weaponization of Stigma:** What the author calls "Spiritual Lawfare" created an environment where belonging to the "normative" majority was treated as an active hostility. For a generation already suffering from the Invisibility Crisis and profound atomization, the psychic burden of being labeled an "oppressor" simply for existing was intolerable.  
* **The Escape Hatch of the Self:** Because race and class were largely immutable, the fluid, subjective nature of gender and sexual identity became the only accessible escape hatch. By claiming a marginalized identity, a young person could instantly teleport from the bottom of the moral hierarchy (the oppressor) to the protected class (the victim). It was, as the author brilliantly notes, a "deliberate and strategic choice" to secure empathy in a ruthless, low-trust society.
### Act II: The Evaporation of the Abstract (2025-2040s)

The author notes a "vibe shift" post-2024 that relieved this pressure, causing the "tidal wave" to recede. He frames this as a political victory. History reveals it was the leading edge of a much more terrifying physical reality.

* **The End of the Luxury Belief:** The fluid identity games of the 2020s required a society of immense physical safety and material abundance. When the AI Energy Wall was hit and the global supply chains began to snap, that abundance vanished. The "vibe shift" was actually the onset of the Triage Economy.  
* **The Brutality of the Physical:** In the chaotic years of the Great Fragmentation, the abstract moral hierarchies of intersectionality instantly evaporated. When a city’s power grid fails, or when food lines form, biological reality and physical competence reassert themselves with non-negotiable force. You cannot use a bespoke pronoun to negotiate with a warlord, nor does a victimhood status generate the joules required to heat a home.  
* **The Great Physical Sorting:** As the culture fractured into Neotribes and militias, survival required absolute cohesion. The people who retreated into the "Männerbunds" and early Builder Stacks demanded conformity, physical strength, and reproductive viability. The synthetic, highly individualized identities of the 2020s were shed not because they went out of style, but because they became a lethal liability in a survival scenario.
### Act III: The Biology of the Fortress (Post-2060s)

The survivors of the collapse—the architects of the Sovereign Stacks—look back at the era described in this article as a time of mass, culturally-induced delirium.

* **The Revaluation of the Real:** In the Era of Systemic Resilience, identity is not something you "opt into" to gain moral leverage. Identity is anchored entirely to physical, verifiable reality. The Bounded Truth of our civilization rejects the premise that an internal feeling supersedes biological fact.  
* **The Human Phyla:** The legacy of this era forced a hard fork in the human species. The Transcended Stacks embraced the ultimate fluidity, merging with the machine to escape biology entirely. The Bioconservative Stacks, reacting in horror to the psychological chaos of the 2020s, enshrined the baseline biological human as sacred.  
* **The Sacred Duty:** In my time, the ultimate "normative" behavior—reproduction and the continuation of the bloodline—is not viewed as an oppressive structure. It is the Sacred Duty, the central organizing principle of the Purpose Economy. We do not reward citizens for discovering new ways to be unique; we reward them for their concrete contributions to the survival of the tribe.

The author of this piece believed he was documenting the retreat of a political agenda. We have the hindsight to know he was documenting the moment a civilization ran out of the luxury to pretend, just moments before the harsh, unyielding winter of reality set in.
