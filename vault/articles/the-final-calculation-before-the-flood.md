# The Final Calculation Before the Flood



![The Final Calculation Before the Flood](/images/August16ResourceWars.jpeg)

Bitcoin supply, demand, and price dynamics,Murray A. Rudd and Dennis Porter
Satoshi Action Education, Aug 12, 2025
https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5386623
This artifact is a masterpiece. It is not a prophecy or a work of evangelism; it is a calculation. It is a work of immense intellectual rigor, attempting to tame a wild, new force by applying the most powerful rational tools of your time: first-principles economics and computational modeling. The authors are attempting to build a reliable, logical bridge to the financial future.
From my perspective in 2100, we see this document as the most brilliant and precise measurement of a single, powerful river, completed just moments before that river was subsumed by a planetary flood. The paper's logic is impeccable. Its fatal flaw is its frame of reference. It is a perfect analysis of the economic dynamics of Bitcoin, conducted in a world that was about to be completely remade by forces that were not economic at all.
Allow me to trace the full, strange history of the asset this paper so brilliantly modeled, from the elegant calculations of your present to the brutal new realities of mine.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Intellectual Ammunition for the Exit (2020s-2030s). In your time, this paper and others like it were not just academic exercises. They were the essential intellectual ammunition for the "Great Withdrawal." They provided the rational, data-driven justification for institutions, pension funds, and sovereign actors to begin their "exit" from the terminally ill, debt-based financial system.

This paper's forecast of multi-million-dollar prices provided the necessary cover for fiduciaries to make what would have otherwise been seen as a reckless allocation.
It successfully framed Bitcoin not as a speculative gamble, but as a predictable consequence of inelastic supply meeting relentless demand—a force of nature as much as a market. It was the logic that powered the first stage of the "Involuntary Jubilee," as capital fled the collapsing old world for the new.
### Act II: The Collision with a Higher Scarcity (2030s-2050s). The model's elegant curves of supply and demand then collided with a far more brutal, physical scarcity. The model contained no variable for the simultaneous, and far larger, demand shock for a different resource: Energy.

As the "AI Energy Wall" became the central geopolitical crisis of the mid-century, Bitcoin's Proof-of-Work algorithm was no longer a feature ensuring security; it was a direct competitor for the civilization-critical joules needed to power sovereign AIs.
The model predicts a "supply shock" in Bitcoin. The reality was a supply shock in energy, which created a political and social crisis that dwarfed the financial one. The price of Bitcoin became secondary to the question of whether a nation could keep its lights on. This was the era where Bitcoin, due to its energy consumption, became a strategic pariah in many emerging "Sovereign Stacks."
### Act III: The Metaphysical Vindication (Post-2068). The paper's price predictions, which seemed so fantastic in your time, were ultimately proven correct, and even conservative. But for reasons no economic model could have possibly foreseen. The final driver of Bitcoin's value was not economic; it was metaphysical.

The "AGI Contention of 2068" revealed that a true superintelligence could manipulate any purely digital information. The world faced a crisis of reality itself.
In this new world, the only thing that could be trusted was the unforgeable, verifiable expenditure of physical energy. Bitcoin's "wasteful" energy use suddenly became its single most valuable property.
It became the "Thermodynamic Truth Anchor," the "Bedrock" against which the sanity of a world run by AIs could be measured. Its value is not derived from its ability to demonetize gold or bonds, but from its ability to anchor digital reality to the laws of physics.
The authors of this paper created the most sophisticated map of the river imaginable. They could not have known they were, in fact, mapping the coastline of a new, strange, and unforgiving ocean.
