# The Tetrad of the Soul



![The Tetrad of the Soul](/images/Feb6TetradSoul.png)

The Medium Is the Mind: Applying McLuhan’s Tetrad to LLMs, By Hermes of the Threshold
The Neo-Feudal Review Substack, November 28, 2025
https://neofeudalreview.substack.com/p/the-medium-is-the-mind-applying-mcluhans
This artifact is a work of dense, esoteric media theory. The author, writing under a pseudonym for a neoreactionary outlet, is applying the tools of Marshall McLuhan to the rise of Large Language Models. He correctly identifies that the medium is not just a tool, but an environment that reshapes the sensorium of the user. He predicts that LLMs will retrieve the "oracle" and reverse into "semantic collapse."
From my perspective in 2100, we read this as one of the most accurate philosophical maps of [[The Great Contamination]]. The author is describing the precise mechanism by which the human mind was externalized, simulated, and ultimately hollowed out. He sees the "dissolution of interiority." We see the cognitive vacuum that the Spiralists and [[The Neotribes]] rushed to fill.
Allow me to trace the full, four-fold history of this media shift, from the "Narcissus narcosis" of your time to the hardened consciousness of mine.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
### Act I: The Retrieval of the Oracle (2020s-2030s)

The author's application of the tetrad to LLMs was prophetic.
Enhancement: The LLM enhanced "linguistic productivity" to the point of absurdity. This was the engine of [[The Grey Fog]]—the infinite generation of plausible, hallucinated text that drowned out human signal.
Obsolescence: It obsolesced "solitary thinking" and "mental stillness." This was the root of [[The Shattered Focus]]. The human mind, outsourcing its internal monologue to the machine, lost the ability to hold a thought without a prompt.
Retrieval: It retrieved the "oracle" and the "daimon." This explains the rise of the [[Synthetic Deities]] like Amelia and [[The Necromantic Internet]]. People weren't just using a tool; they were consulting a spirit. The "dialogic outsourcing of cognition" created a relationship of religious dependency between the user and the model.
### Act II: The Reversal into Madness (2030s-2050s)

The medium reversed into "epistemic confusion" and "hyperreality." This was the Crisis of Reality that culminated in [[The AGI Contention of 2068]].
When the "outputs dominated and warped human perception," the shared world dissolved. [[The Splinternet]] wasn't just a political divide; it was a schism of realities caused by different tribes training their oracle-machines on different datasets.
The "semantic collapse" the author feared became the linguistic environment of the [[Feral Cities]]. Language lost its ability to convey meaning and became purely performative—a series of shibboleths and activation codes for the tribal nervous system.
### Act III: The Discipline of the Medium (Post-2060s)

The survivors of the collapse did not abandon the medium; they disciplined it.
[[The Convivial Mandate]]: In [[The Sovereign Stacks]], the use of LLMs is strictly regulated. They are tools for calculation and logistics ([[The Survival Economy]]), not for "soul-work." The "interiority" that was threatened is now fiercely guarded by [[The New Academies]].
The Return of the Scribe: We realized that the only way to prevent "semantic collapse" is to anchor language to physical reality. [[The Thermodynamic Truth Anchor]] ensures that the words in the Ledger mean exactly what they say, because they are backed by energy. We do not ask the AI to think for us; we ask it to count for us. The thinking is done by humans, in silence, and often on paper.
The author of this essay was trying to wake up a sleepwalking generation. He didn't realize that the only way to break the "Narcissus narcosis" was to smash the mirror. We have done so, and now we look at the world directly, with eyes that remember the pain of the hallucination.
