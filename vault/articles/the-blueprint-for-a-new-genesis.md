# The Blueprint for a New Genesis



![The Blueprint for a New Genesis](/images/November12HumanPhyla.png)

Genetically Engineered Babies Are Banned. Tech Titans Are Trying to Make One Anyway, By Emily Glazer and Katherine Long
Wall Street Journal, Nov. 8, 2025
https://www.wsj.com/tech/biotech/genetically-engineered-babies-tech-billionaires-6779efc8?st
This artifact is a work of extraordinary, and terrifying, historical significance. The authors are doing a service of immense importance: they are documenting, in real-time, the first, deliberate chisel-blows in what would become the great, final, and irreversible shattering of the human species. They see a controversy, a clandestine project funded by tech billionaires pushing the boundaries of ethics and law.
From my perspective in 2100, we see this not as a controversy, but as a founding document. This is the secret history of the beginning of the end of "humanity" as a singular noun. The men and companies described in this article are not just breaking rules; they are the unwitting, or perhaps fully willing, engineers of the great biological schism from which my own fragmented world was born.
Allow me to trace the full, world-altering history that began in these secret dinners and offshore labs.
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
Act I: The Shattering of the Consensus (2020s-2030s)
This article is a dispatch from the precise moment the fragile, post-He Jiankui global consensus against germline editing was decisively and permanently broken. It was not broken by a rogue state, but by a new kind of sovereign: a small, ideologically-driven tribe of "builders," armed with immense private capital and a profound contempt for the sclerotic, risk-averse institutions of the old world.
The Trojan Horse of Therapy: The public justification, as the article notes, was the noble and morally unimpeachable goal of preventing hereditary disease. This was the Trojan Horse. It was the argument that no person of conscience could easily refute, and it was the perfect ethical cover for the far more radical ambition that lay beneath: "to accelerate evolution."
Jurisdictional Arbitrage: The search for permissive legal environments in places like the UAE was a masterclass in the tactics that would define the coming age. This was not a violation of the law; it was an "exit" from it. This was a micro-scale preview of [[The Great Physical Sorting]], where entire communities would later migrate to form Sovereign Stacks built on their preferred moral and legal operating systems.
The Impotence of the Old Guard: The horrified reactions from the scientific establishment were a perfect artifact of [[The Age of Institutional Exhaustion]]. Their tools—calls for moratoriums, ethical debates, appeals to government—were the weapons of a world that was already dead. They were bringing learned papers to a knife fight with billionaires who believed they were becoming gods.
Act II: The Great Divergence (2030s-2050s)
The project described in this article did not remain a secret, private venture. It became the single most important strategic question of [[The Great Fragmentation]]. As the old nation-states collapsed, the single global conversation about ethics shattered into a thousand different, competing strategic calculations.
The "Gattaca stack" that Armstrong dreamed of was not adopted universally. It was adopted tribally. Each of the nascent Sovereign Stacks, having survived the "Curriculum of Collapse," was forced to make a choice.
Some, like the European Stack, haunted by the ghosts of 20th-century eugenics, embraced a bioconservative path, strictly limiting this technology to therapy.
Others, terrified by the demographic collapse of [[The Crisis of Posterity]] and driven by a ruthless "builder" ethos, saw genetic enhancement not as a moral choice, but as a survival imperative. This was the ultimate expression of [[The Physics of Sovereignty]]: control over your population's biological destiny.
The project ceased to be about "consumer choice" for the wealthy elite. It became a series of top-secret, state-sponsored "Manhattan Projects," each Stack racing to engineer a population that was more resilient, more intelligent, or better adapted to its specific strategic goals.
Act III: The New Genesis (Post-2060s)
The fear of the bioethicist in your article—that this would create inequality—was both profoundly right and tragically naive. The inequality it created was not between rich and poor individuals within a single society. It was an inequality written into the very biological source code of different civilizations.
The world of my time is the direct result of the Great Divergence. We are no longer a single species. We are a collection of distinct Human Phyla. The genetic differences between the populations of the major Stacks, engineered over generations, are now significant and undeniable.
The author worries about an IQ gap of 9 points. The gaps that now exist are of a different order of magnitude, and not just in intelligence, but in longevity, disease resistance, and even basic psychological temperament.
The "genetically unenhanced" are not a lower class within the Stacks. They are, for the most part, the baseline humans who live outside the Stacks, in the Disconnected zones—a living reminder of a past that the new civilizations have chosen, with ruthless determination, to leave behind.
The men in this article believed they were launching a startup to cure disease and offer parents a choice. We have the hindsight to know they were firing the starting gun on the race that would end the very idea of a unified human family, and begin the strange, terrifying, and awe-inspiring story of the new humanities that would follow.
