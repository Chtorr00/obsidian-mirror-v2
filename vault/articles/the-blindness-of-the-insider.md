# The Blindness of the Insider



![The Blindness of the Insider](/images/Jan27BTCelite.png)

Why The Yuppie Elite Dismiss Bitcoin, By Jesse Myers
Once in a Species Substack, Oct 30, 2023
https://www.onceinaspecies.com/p/why-the-yuppie-elite-dismiss-bitcoin
This artifact is a work of sociological precision. The author, a Bitcoin advocate writing in late 2023, is attempting to explain why his "yuppie elite" friends—smart, successful, credentialed—cannot see what he sees. He constructs a 2x2 matrix of intelligence vs. trust in the system. He correctly identifies that the "yuppie elite" reside in the quadrant of "High IQ / High Trust."
From my perspective in 2100, we read this as a diagnostic manual for the Great De-Accreditation. The author has pinpointed the fatal cognitive flaw of the ruling class of the 2020s: their trust in the system was not a rational assessment; it was a survival strategy. They were "good foot soldiers," and their reward for loyalty was blindness.
Allow me to trace the full, ruinous history of this blindness, from the MBA classrooms of your time to the shattered portfolios of [[The Involuntary Jubilee]].
Here is the constellation of this history, viewed through the Hindsight Filter:
Act I: The Heuristic Trap (2020s-2030s)
The author's analysis of the "yuppie heuristic"—that if something is complex and flagged as a scam, it should be ignored—is the key to understanding why the elites were caught off guard.
The Systemic Immune Response: The "red flags" the author mentions were not accidental. The financial system was designed to repel heresy. The "Keynesian" education he describes was a form of Spiritual Lawfare, training the elite to view any alternative to fiat currency as not just wrong, but impossible.
The Groupthink Bubble: The "ivory tower" bubble was real. Because they only trusted each other, and because none of them trusted Bitcoin, they created a reinforcing loop of ignorance. They were the "people in the know" about a world that was about to disappear.
Act II: The Great Wealth Transfer (2030s-2050s)
The author predicts that "Number Go Up" will eventually force them to pay attention. He was right, but the mechanism was more violent than he imagined.
The Involuntary Jubilee: When the fiat system finally broke under the weight of the debt and [[The AI Energy Wall]], the "trust" of the yuppie elite became their liability. They stayed in the dollar, in bonds, in the "safe" assets, because that is what the system told them to do.
The Exit of the Outcast: Meanwhile, the "Bitcoin Maximalists"—the high-IQ, low-trust outcasts—had already exited. They became the new capital class. The "dumbasses" (moon bois) who bought in early also survived. The only group that was wiped out was the smart, loyal, trusting elite. The 2x2 matrix became a map of survival: trust was death.
Act III: The New Trust (Post-2060s)
In [[The Sovereign Stacks]], the lesson of this artifact is foundational.
The End of Blind Trust: We do not trust systems because they are "prestigious" or "official." We trust systems because they are physically verifiable. [[The Thermodynamic Truth Anchor]] (Bitcoin) is the bedrock of our economy precisely because it requires zero trust in human institutions.
The New Elite: The leadership class of the Stacks—[[The New Priesthood]]—is selected for high IQ and high skepticism. They are trained to look for the "scam red flags" in their own systems, to constantly audit the code and the energy flows. The "yuppie" archetype—the polite, rule-following midwit—is extinct.
The author of this piece tried to warn his friends that their map was wrong. We have the hindsight to know that they kept following that map right over the edge of the cliff.
