# The Neotribes

**Timeline:** 2025 - 2050

The emergent social organizations that filled the power vacuum left by the dying nation-state. Unlike traditional political parties, Neotribes were high-cohesion, low-scale groups bound by shared aesthetics, digital myths (egregores), or survival needs. They ranged from the Virtual Koryos (digital war-bands) to local mutual-aid networks. During the Great Fragmentation, these loose associations hardened into the armed, territorial entities that eventually founded the Sovereign Stacks.
