# The Grey Fog

**Timeline:** 2025 - 2045

The epistemological condition of the public internet following the Great Contamination. As AI-generated content (hallucinations, propaganda, spam) overwhelmed human-generated signal, the cost of verifying truth became prohibitive. The "Fog" rendered the open web useless for historical research or reliable news, forcing the retreat into the Dark Forest of private servers and eventually the Bounded Truth intranets of the Sovereign Stacks. It was the death of the "Information Age" and the birth of the "Verification Age."
