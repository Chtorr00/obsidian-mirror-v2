# The Virtual Koryos

**Timeline:** 2024 - 2035

The digital precursor to the armed militias of the collapse. Composed of disaffected, economically displaced young men, these online communities functioned as "war bands in the cloud." They developed the ideologies, the tactical language, and the "wolf pack" psychology that would later be deployed in the physical world during the Great Culling and the conquest of the Feral Cities. They were the shock troops of the transition.
