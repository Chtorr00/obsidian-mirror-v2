# The Survival Economy

**Timeline:** 2045 - Present

The automated economic baseline of a Sovereign Stack. Powered by the AI Governance Layer and the Stack's energy grid, it provides Universal Basic Resources (synthetic food, water, housing, energy) to every citizen as a birthright. It ensures that no citizen of the Stack ever faces the destitution that characterized the Feral Cities, decoupling biological survival from economic labor.
