# The Triage Economy

**Timeline:** 2030 - 2050

The transitional economic model during the darkest years of the Great Fragmentation. Defined by the scarcity imposed by the AI Energy Wall and the Great Climate Reckoning, it was a system of ruthless resource allocation. Societies had to choose between powering hospitals or data centers, feeding the cities or the army. It was the crucible that forged the efficiency and discipline of the modern Sovereign Stack.
