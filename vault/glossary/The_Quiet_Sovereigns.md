# The Quiet Sovereigns

**Timeline:** 2030 - Present

The populations who survived the collapse by refusing to engage with it. These are the traditionalist, often agrarian communities who maintained 19th-century technologies and social structures. By ignoring the Dopamine Trap and the AI Energy Wall, they remained robust while the high-tech world burned. They are viewed with a mix of suspicion and envy by the citizens of the Stacks.
