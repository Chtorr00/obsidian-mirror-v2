# Cognitive Quarantine

**Timeline:** 2030 - 2055

The emergency measure implemented by early Stacks to protect their populations from Cognitive Warfare and the Great Contamination. It involved severing connections to the global internet, banning unauthorized data streams, and physically isolating the citizenry from "viral" memes or ideologies. It was the precursor to the permanent system of Bounded Truth.
