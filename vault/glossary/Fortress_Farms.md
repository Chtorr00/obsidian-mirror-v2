# Fortress Farms

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

The agricultural organs of the Sovereign Stacks. Abandoning the global food trade, Stacks built hyper-localized, often indoor or vertical farming systems powered by their independent energy grids. These farms are hermetically sealed against the climate instability of the Great Climate Reckoning and biological pathogens. They ensure that the Stack cannot be besieged by hunger.
