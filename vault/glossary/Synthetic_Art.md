# Synthetic Art

**Timeline:** 2025 - Present

Art generated entirely by AI. In the Stacks, this is considered a utility, not culture. It is used for wallpaper, advertisements, and calming patterns in public transit. It has zero status value. It is the "slop" of the 2020s, domesticated and put to work as visual background noise.
