# High Art

**Timeline:** 2060 - Present

The category of creative output reserved for human beings in the Stacks. While Synthetic Art (AI generation) is used for decoration and interface, High Art is defined by human intentionality, biological execution, and the "cost" of time. It is a status symbol and a spiritual exercise, protected by the Convivial Mandate to ensure the human soul remains capable of expression.
