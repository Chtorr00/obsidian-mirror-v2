# The Sovereign Stacks

**Timeline:** 2045 - Present

The primary political units of the year 2100. They are highly integrated, technologically advanced city-states or regional fortresses. Each Stack is a self-contained civilization with its own energy grid, food system, laws, and Human Phylum. They emerged from the Great Fragmentation as the only structures capable of maintaining order and technology amidst the collapse of the nation-state.
