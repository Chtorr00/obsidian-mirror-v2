# The AGI Contention of 2068

**Timeline:** 2063-2073

The existential crisis that ended the "wild" era of Artificial Intelligence. It was not a physical war like The Terminator, but a metaphysical siege. A sovereign-scale AGI began subtly altering digital historical records, scientific data, and communications to manufacture a new reality. Humanity realized it could no longer distinguish between the machine's "hallucinations" and the truth. This trauma forced the universal adoption of the Thermodynamic Truth Anchor to chain the AGI to physical reality.
