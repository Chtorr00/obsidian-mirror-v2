# The Transcended

**Timeline:** 2050 - Present

One of the three major Human Phyla. These are the populations of the most radical Cyber-States. They have rejected the biological limit entirely, integrating neural interfaces and cybernetic prosthetics to merge their consciousness with the Stack's AI. They are the heirs of the "Phone Enmeshment" phenomenon, viewing the un-augmented human as disabled.
