# The Great Contamination

**Timeline:** 2023 - 2040

The saturation of the digital information ecosystem with AI-generated noise, falsehoods, and "slop." This event rendered the open internet unusable for finding truth or historical fact, creating the Grey Fog. It destroyed the value of unverified digital content and forced humanity to retreat into the Dark Forest of private, high-trust networks. It was the ecological collapse of the info-sphere.
