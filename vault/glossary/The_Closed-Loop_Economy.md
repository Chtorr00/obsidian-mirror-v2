# The Closed-Loop Economy

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

The economic survival strategy that emerged during the Great Fragmentation. Unlike the globalized, linear supply chains of the 20th century (extraction -> production -> waste), a Closed-Loop Economy is designed for total autarky within a Sovereign Stack. It utilizes advanced recycling, synthetic biology, and local manufacturing to ensure that the Stack can survive indefinitely without imports. It is the economic expression of the Physics of Sovereignty: if you cannot make it or recycle it within your walls, you do not own it.
