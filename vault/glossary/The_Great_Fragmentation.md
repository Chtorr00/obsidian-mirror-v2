# The Great Fragmentation

**Timeline:** 2028 - 2050

The macro-historical event of the mid-21st century. It describes the shattering of the globalized world order into a mosaic of competing, distinct, and often hostile entities. It includes the breakdown of global trade, the end of universal internet connectivity (Splinternet), and the divergence of the human species into different cultural and biological pathways. It is the transition point between the "One World" of the past and the "Many Worlds" of the present.
