# The Builder Stacks

**Timeline:** 2035 - Present

The successor states to the "Red" or "Freedom" enclaves of the pre-collapse era (e.g., Texas, Florida). Founded by the "Architects" who fled the coastal cities during the Great Physical Sorting, these Stacks prioritize industrial capacity, energy dominance, and the Physics of Sovereignty. They are characterized by deregulation of the physical world (allowing rapid construction of nuclear plants and factories) combined with strict social conservatism. They are the industrial engines of the new world.
